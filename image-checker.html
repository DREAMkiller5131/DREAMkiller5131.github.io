Maniké — Image Path Checker

Open this file from your website folder (same place as index.html). It checks whether your product images are in the right place and will be visible on your live site.

Step 1 — Load your product data

Click the button to read your products from the admin panel's localStorage. This only works if you open this file in the same browser you use for admin.html.

Step 2 — Check image paths

For images to show on your live site, each file must exist in the correct folder:
• Collection/Unique pages: images/filename.jpg (next to index.html)
• Product detail pages: images/filename.jpg (same — product pages use ../images/)

Folder structure guide

Your website folder should look like this:
📁 your-website-folder/
  ├── index.html
  ├── collection.html
  ├── unique.html
  ├── story.html
  ├── contact.html
  ├── admin.html
  ├── 📁 images/    ← all product photos go here
  │    ├── ring-01.jpg
  │    └── earrings-02.jpg
  ├── 📁 slideshow/  ← homepage background photos
  └── 📁 products/   ← individual product pages
        └── moonstone-ring.html
