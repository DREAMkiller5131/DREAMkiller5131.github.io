Jewelry made to carry meaning

Maniké was born from a belief that the most beautiful things are those made slowly, with intention — and that a piece of jewelry should feel like it was made for you alone.

Where it began

Maniké started with a fascination for gemstones — the way light moves through a moonstone, the depth of colour in a raw sapphire, the warmth of a hand-polished garnet.

Each piece begins with a stone chosen for its character, not its perfection.

The craft

Every Maniké piece is made by hand. No casting lines, no machine-stamped settings. The marks of the maker are part of the design.

We work primarily in sterling silver — a material that ages beautifully, holds fine detail, and lets the gemstone remain the focus.

Unique pieces

Some pieces in our collection are made once, and once only. A stone that will never be found again, a form that came together in a single session at the bench.

When it's gone, it's gone — and that rarity is the point.
